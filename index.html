Method & assumptions
Lumen method E = (N·Φ·UF·MF)/A; UF from room cavity ratio + reflectances. Heatmap: per-fixture inverse-square × cosine, normalised so grid mean = lumen-method electric avg, then daylight added uniformly. UGR is a CIE-style screening estimate (source luminance, count, mounting height, background). Daylight via average daylight-factor method.
